Hello there

I'm Adil Aboulkacim, a student currently studying 5th and last year of MSc in computer science in Mid Sweden University.

I'm enthusiastic about tech in coding and learning new things about computers and what they can do. I've been dabbling with coding since roughly age of 15, until I started studying computer science at university at 19 where my interest really peaked. Take a peek at the projects I have done, or jump straight to my github. My free time is sometimes contributing to open source projects and answer questions in forums and helping people with code.

Other than coding I enjoy tinkering with computers and my homelab, as of now includes an old PC running Raid 1 TrueNAS and a Raspberry Pi 3 running Pi-hole and PiVPN. I also love to attended hackathons where people and code meet together to solve solutions in a hastly manner.
